About

The Registrar’s Office of URS Morong Campus serves as the central unit responsible for managing all academic and enrollment-related processes. Our office ensures that student records are accurately maintained, requests are processed efficiently, and academic services are delivered with transparency and integrity. We aim to support every student’s academic journey from admission to graduation.

We continuously improve our services by adopting modern systems and maintaining open communication with students, faculty, and staff. Our commitment is to make the academic and administrative processes seamless, reliable, and student-friendly.
